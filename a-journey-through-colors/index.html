By Jorlanny Colon

This interactive sketch uses colors and movement to express emotions. Click anywhere on the canvas to change the color and watch how the behavior of the shape changes. Each color represents a different emotion:

  • Red (Angry): Erratic, rapid movements to show intensity and frustration.
  • Blue (Sad): Slow, downward drifting to reflect a heavy, somber mood.
  • Green (Calm): Smooth bouncing to convey relaxation and steadiness.
  • Yellow (Excited): Rapid size changes to show energy and enthusiasm.

Use your mouse to change the shape's color and your keyboard to adjust its size. Have fun exploring the emotions!
